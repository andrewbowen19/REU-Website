Other Interests/Projects

In addition to astronomy, I am also very passionate about sports. I am a broadcaster and writer for Northwestern's WNUR Sports radio station. This extra-curricular has given me the opportunity to travel to some great college campuses to broadcast Northwestern sports on radio. As an extension of my love for sports, I also have a side project in which I am trying to apply network theory to basketball offenses.

I have also been studying Italian for over two years now. I am not completely fluent yet, but hope to be when I return from study abroad at the University of Bologna this Fall of 2018.

When I am not at Northwestern, you can normally find me trapsing around the Pocono Mountains in Pennsylavnia, where I currently call 'home.' I am an avid fisherman and backpacker with a (some would say unhealthy) love of the outdoors.
