About

I am currently an undergraduate student at Northwestern University majoring in Physics & Astronomy, with a minor in italian. During Summer 2018, I worked under the supervision of Aaron M. Geller as part of the CIERA REU program. I worked on the filter system of the new Large Synoptic Survey Telescope (LSST).

My focus was finding new limb-darkening coefficients for the new y-filter used on the LSST. After finding them, I was able to run simulations of these eclipsing binary stars to see how efficiently ewe can recover the period with these new limb-darkening coefficients.
